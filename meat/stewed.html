Stewed Chicken with Chestnuts

Recipe Details:
Level of difficulties
Serving 3 pax
Cooking duration 30 minutes
Nutritional Facts:
Calories 294 kJ

Ingredients

  1. 1 chicken
  2. 225g chestnuts (shelled)
  3. 2 sprigs spring onion (sectioned)
  4. 5 sliced ginger
  5. 1 tsp chopped garlic
  6. 2 tbsp wine
  7. 2 tbsp oil
  8. 1/2 tsp salt
  9. 1 tsp sugar
  10. 2 tsps light soy sauce
  11. 1/2 tbsp oyster sauce
  12. 1½ cups water
  13. 3/4 tsp corn flour
  14. 2 tbsps water
  15. Dash of sesame oil

Method

  1. Rinse and chop up chicken.
  2. Soak chestnuts in boiling water and peel.
  3. Heat oil in a wok. Stir fry chestnuts for a while. Set aside.
  4. Stir fry ginger, garlic and chicken. Sprinkle wine. Add chestnuts and seasoning. Bring to the boil.
  5. Stew over medium heat till chicken and chestnuts are tender.
  6. Put in spring onion and sauce. Mix well and serve hot.
