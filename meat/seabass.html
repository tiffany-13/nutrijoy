Baked Sea Bass with Lemon Grass

Recipe Details:
Level of difficulties
Serving 2-3 pax
Cooking duration 1 hour
Nutritional Facts:
Calories 298 kJ

Ingredients

  1. 1 sea bass (about 675g/1½ lb, cleaned and scaled)
  2. 30 ml/2 tbsp olive oil
  3. 2 lemon grass stalks (finely sliced)
  4. 1 red onion (finely shredded)
  5. 1 fresh red chilli (seeded and finely chopped)
  6. 5 cm/2 in piece fresh root ginger (finely shredded)
  7. 45 ml/3 tbsp chopped fish coriander (cilantro)
  8. Pared rind and juice of 2 limes
  9. 30 ml/2 tbsp light soy sauce
  10. Salt
  11. Ground black pepper

Method

  1. Soak a fish clay pot in cold water for 20 minutes, then drain.
  2. Make 4-5 diagonal slashes on both sides of the fish. Repeat the slashes on one side in the opposite direction to give an attractive cross-hatched effect.
  3. Rub the sea bass inside and with salt, pepper and 15 ml/1 tbsp of olive oil.
  4. Mix the sliced lemon grass, red onion, chilli, ginger, coriander and lime rind in a bowl.
  5. Place a little of the lemon grass and red onion mixture in the base of the clay pot, then lay the fish on top.
  6. Sprinkle the remaining mixture over the fish, then sprinkle over the lime juice, soy sauce and the remaining olive oil. Cover and place in an unheated oven.
  7. Set the oven to 220°C/425°F/Gas 7 and cook the fish for 30-40 minutes, or until the flash flakes easily when tested with a knife. Serve hot.
