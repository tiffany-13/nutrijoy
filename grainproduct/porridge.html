Ba Bao Porridge

Recipe Details:
Level of difficulties
Serving 4 pax
Cooking duration 60 minutes
Nutritional Facts:
Calories 200 kJ

Ingredients

  1. Red beans - 2 tbsp
  2. Red beans - 2 tbsp
  3. Kidney beans - 2 tbsp
  4. Black beans- 2 tbsp
  5. Black or purple rice - 2 tbsp
  6. Glutinous rice - 1/4 cup
  7. Peanuts - 1/4 cups
  8. Chestnuts - 5-8
  9. Walnuts (quartered) - 2
  10. Dried lily bulbs (bai he) - 2 tbsp
  11. Dried or fresh Chinese yam (Shan Yao) - 1-2 slices
  12. Lotus seeds (lian zi) - 8-10
  13. Chinese red dates - 8-10
  14. Goji berries - 8-10
  15. Chinese pearl barley (yi ren) - 2 tbsp

Method

  1. Pre-soak beans overnight and soak the glutinous rice for about four hours.
  2. Place all the ingredients in a large stock pot with 12 cups of water.
  3. Leave a gap between the top of the water and the pot cover to prevent the congee from boiling over.
  4. Bring to boil, turn the heat to low, cover and simmer for 40 to 60 minutes until all the ingredients are soft to taste. Serve hot.
