Grain Products

Egg Fried Rice
Difficulties:
Serving: 4 pax
Duration: 15 minutes
Short Description: The rice is mixed with colorful vegetables like corn, green beans, and diced red chilies, giving it a vibrant appearance. On top of the rice, there's a halved soft-boiled egg garnished with a sprig of fresh cilantro, adding a finishing touch to the dish. The steam rising from the plate suggests it's freshly prepared and ready to enjoy.
Ba Bao Porridge
Difficulties:
Serving: 4 pax
Duration: 60 minutes
Short Description: The porridge has a rich, deep brownish-red color, indicating the use of red beans and grains. It contains a mix of grains, beans, and seeds, giving it a hearty texture. Served in a wooden bowl, the dish is warm and comforting, often enjoyed for its sweet and slightly nutty flavor.
Cantonese Yee Mee
Difficulties:
Serving: 2 pax
Duration: 18 minutes
Short Description: The crispy fried noodles are partially submerged in a savory, thick gravy. The dish is topped with a mix of ingredients, including shrimp, sliced fish cake, leafy green vegetables, and tender slices of meat, creating a hearty and flavorful meal. The combination of the crispy noodles and rich sauce makes for a delightful contrast in textures, a signature of this classic Cantonese dish.
