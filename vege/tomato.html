Tomato Egg Stir-Fry

Recipe Details:
Level of difficulties
Serving 2pax
Cooking Duration 10 mins
Nutritional Facts:
Calories 193kJ

Ingredients:

  1. 3 tbsp oil
  2. 4 egg
  3. lightly beaten with ½ tsp salt and 1/8 tsp pepper
  4. 1 stalk spring onion
  5. white parts chopped and green parts shredded
  6. 3 medium tomatoes, cut into thin wedges
  7. 1/2 tsp sugar

Methods:

  1. Head up 2 tbsp oil in a wok, swirl to coat the base.

  2. Pour in the egg mixture, swirl into a round shape, cook until a thin layer forms at the bottom, then scramble the eggs until they are cooked but still light and fluffy. Dish and set aside.

  3. Heat up another 1 tbsp oil, add the spring onion whites and stir-fry until aromatic.

  4. Add the tomatoes and cook for 1 to 2 minutes, until the juices start to release and the tomatoes wedges are slightly softened but still intact. Sprinkle sugar over the tomatoes and mix well.

  5. Return the eggs to the pan, stir to cook for another minute until well-mixed with the tomatoes.

  6. Dish onto a serving plate and sprinkle the shredded spring onion on top as garnishing. Serve hot.
