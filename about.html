Origin of passion:

From a young age, she developed a deep appreciation for Chinese cuisine, influenced by her family's rich culinary traditions.

Current Role:

She is now sharing healthy, easy-to-cook Chinese recipes through her website and social media platforms. Her recipes are prefect for health-conscious individuals, blending authentic Chinese flavours with modern dietary needs.



Chef in Black Sesame Restaurant, Beijing

Amy Chong Lin worked and gained experience in both traditional Chinese cuisine and modern culinary trends. Her work toned her skilled and push boundaries in Chiense cooking, earning her a reputation as a rising star in the culinary world.

Culinary Intership in Chenmopo Sichuan Restaurant , Chengdu

She worked under a renowed Sichanese master chef, who taught her delicate balance of spice and flavor that makes Sichuan cuisine famous. She not only mastered the bold, fiery dished like mapo tofu and dan dan noodles but also learnd the healing properties of traditional Chinese herbs.

Winner of "Healthy Chef Challenge"

She beat chefs from around the world by stunning the judges with her Sichuan-style quinoa stir-fry, a dish that blended Western superfoods with Chinese flavors. Winning the challenge helped her gain international recognition and inspired her to learn more about cooking.

Dietitian in Beijing United Family Hospital and Clinics

She helped clients manages thier diets, blending her culinary expertise with nutritional knowledge to create meals that are as healthy as they are delicious.
